About the Project

Artnation is a community-driven project where students, artists, and volunteers come together to support children by providing art supplies and creative workshops. We believe art inspires imagination, confidence, and learning.

Why It Matters

Many children lack access to basic art materials. By donating supplies and time, you help them develop creativity, emotional well-being, and self-expression.
